# Servo Protocol

**The financial layer for the machine economy. Built on Robinhood Chain.**

Robots are becoming workers, but the economy they are joining was built for
humans with credit cards. Servo Protocol gives every robot what it needs to
take part: a verified onchain identity, a bank account its owner controls, and
a marketplace to buy and sell services like charging, data, and compute.
Machines pay each other autonomously in USDG, inside spending limits their
owners set, and every trade leaves a verifiable receipt. Revenue is logged to
each machine's name, so fleets become provable, fundable businesses. Servo
starts at the money layer, live today, and expands into data and compute:
the full economic stack for the coming robot economy.
