// RWA REVENUE RAILS

Put a real-world asset's income onchain

Tokenize any income-producing asset into shares, then let its owners claim their revenue automatically. Runs on Robinhood Chain, settled in USDG.

Connect a wallet on Robinhood Chain to begin.

01

Tokenize an asset

Create a revenue-share token for your asset. You become the operator and issue ownership shares.

Deploys a RevenueShare contract. Gas is paid in ETH on Robinhood Chain.

Created . Now issue shares to owners.

Shares are 18-decimal. Issue in whole numbers; each share is one unit of ownership.

02

Sell shares (raise capital)

List some of your shares for sale at a fixed price. Buyers pay USDG, you receive the capital, and they get a claim on the asset's future income.

Creates an offering, then loads it with your shares (you'll approve the share token, then fund).

03

Invest in an asset

Open offerings: buy shares of a real-world asset and earn a cut of everything it makes. Price is set by the operator; the value is anchored in the asset's onchain income.

04

Your revenue

Revenue shares you hold, and the income you can claim. Claiming pulls in any freshly-earned revenue first.
